# 📈 Diversified Equity Portfolio Creation & Performance Analysis

## Overview

This project presents the construction and performance evaluation of a diversified equity portfolio consisting of leading Indian companies across multiple sectors. The objective was to generate superior returns while minimizing risk through strategic diversification, fundamental analysis, and macroeconomic assessment.

The portfolio was designed to benefit from India's domestic growth story while maintaining resilience against global economic uncertainties.

---

## Project Objectives

* Build a diversified portfolio across multiple sectors.
* Reduce unsystematic risk through sector allocation.
* Apply fundamental analysis for stock selection.
* Evaluate portfolio performance against the Sensex benchmark.
* Analyze risk using Beta and volatility measures.
* Understand the impact of macroeconomic and global events on portfolio performance.
